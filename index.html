Welcome

Welcome to Wenli's world, a website for my favorite furball in the whole wide world. She is a little over a year old and she is the sweetest cat you will ever meet. She enjoys being picked up and being carried like a baby, she's an adventourus cat that enjoys the outdoors as she is able to sneak out of the house occasionally but never goes too far, she knows when to sit on your lap to pin you to a chair, especially when you need to do something, she's playful to both human and other pets within the house, and she meows until she finally gets what she wants. She's never a handful and having her around has been the cause for so much happiness for everyone around her, including me. I hope anyone reading this gets to enjoy some of the silly moments that were captured on camera of he silliness, a sneak peak into the daily life of Wenli, my adorable cat.
